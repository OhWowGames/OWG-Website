What is Palettopia?
Palettopia is the relaxing city-painting game where creativity meets harmony. Discover unique islands and paint vibrant districts to bring your dream utopia to life.
Who is making Palettopia?
We are a team of friends and family, willful corporate dropouts, united under a shared passion for art and wholesome creativity.
How can you support the game?
Wishlisting on Steam, signing up for our mailing list, and spreading the word on social media! Every bit of support helps us build a better paradise.
